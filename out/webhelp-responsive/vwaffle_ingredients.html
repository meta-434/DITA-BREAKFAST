Vegan Waffle Ingredients

With this recipe, you would never know it's vegan just by tasting it. The coconut sugar adds a je-ne-sais-quois that makes these waffles a little more special than usual

  1. 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tablespoon coconut sugar
  3. 2 tablespoons baking powder
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 cup almond milk
  6. 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
